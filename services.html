Our Services

Lifting and Safety Equipment Inspections

Our lifting and safety equipment inspection services are designed to ensure the highest level of workplace safety and compliance with industry standards. We conduct rigorous evaluations of critical equipment, including slings, shackles, harnesses, fall arrest systems and more. Using advanced tools and methodologies, we assess wear, damage and functionality to identify potential hazards before they become risks. By prioritising proactive maintenance and compliance checks, our inspections help reduce accidents, enhance operational confidence and maintain adherence to regulations, ensuring your team and operations remain secure.

API CAT III & IV Inspection of Derricks and Substructures

Derricks and substructures play a crucial role in drilling operations and their safety is non-negotiable. Our API CAT III & IV inspections focus on ensuring these components meet the stringent requirements of API 4G standards. Our process involves comprehensive assessments of structural integrity, fatigue and wear using advanced diagnostic tools. Post-inspection, we provide detailed reports with actionable recommendations. These inspections help extend the life of your structures, maintain safety and minimise downtime, ensuring smooth and secure drilling operations.

API 4G Drilling Structure Inspection

We specialise in API 4G Drilling Structure Inspections, covering both Category I and II levels. These inspections are essential for the long-term safety and functionality of your drilling equipment. Our experts thoroughly examine structural components, including joints, welds and critical load-bearing elements, to identify any signs of wear, corrosion, or damage. In addition to conducting inspections, we train your crew to recognise early signs of structural issues, fostering a culture of proactive safety and minimising costly risks.

Dropped Object Surveys

Dropped objects are a leading cause of workplace injuries in industrial environments. Our dropped object surveys are designed to identify and mitigate risks associated with loose, unsecured, or poorly maintained components on rigs and plants. We conduct detailed assessments of vulnerable areas, including elevated workspaces, tools and fixtures, to identify potential hazards. Our team delivers a comprehensive risk report along with actionable recommendations to minimise dropped object incidents, ensuring a safer working environment for your team.

Our pressure testing services utilise high-precision tools and techniques to simulate real-world operating conditions. We assess equipment for leaks, weaknesses, or structural flaws, providing you with a detailed analysis of its readiness for operational use. This service ensures compliance with safety standards, minimises risks and improves reliability, particularly in high-pressure environments.

OCTG Inspections CAT 1-5

Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG) play a vital role in oil and gas operations. Our CAT 1 to CAT 5 OCTG inspections provide comprehensive evaluations of tubular goods, ensuring their integrity and fitness for purpose. We use advanced diagnostic tools to detect issues such as corrosion, cracking and wall thickness variation. Our precise categorisation and analysis help you identify potential weaknesses early, preventing costly failures and enhancing the safety of your operations.
