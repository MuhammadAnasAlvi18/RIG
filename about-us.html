FAQs

RIW Services provides a comprehensive suite of services tailored to the oil, gas, and industrial sectors, including:
  • Rig Inspection and Certification: Conducting thorough API CAT III & IV inspections and LOLER lifting equipment certifications to ensure regulatory compliance and operational safety.
  • Welding and Fabrication: Offering coded welding expertise in high-pressure pipe welding and the fabrication of critical structural elements.
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT): Performing detailed inspections to maintain equipment structural integrity.
  • Equipment Maintenance and Repair: Providing routine and preventive maintenance along with emergency repair services.
  • Other specialized services include Lifting & Safety Equipment Inspections, Dropped Object Surveys, API 4G Drilling Structure Inspection, Rig Conditions Surveys, and more.

RIW Services prioritizes safety by adhering to strict safety protocols and creating risk-free work environments. Their team focuses on quality, efficiency, and accountability, using best practices and the latest technology to deliver timely, reliable results. Post-service evaluations are conducted to ensure all project goals are met.
